Support Animal Sanctuaries!

Why should you care?

Many sanctuaries exist to keep animals protected from formerly neglected lives. The animals come from a variety of places, zoos, circuses, and even just backyard pets. Sanctuaries exist to give all animals who have been harmed a free and happy life.

“We continue to get the calls every day: A family needs to find a home for their "pet" monkey that has become aggressive; endangered antelope bred for captive hunts will be killed by the highest bidder; chimpanzees from laboratories need placement, since they have failed as reliable test subjects for human disease; horses and donkeys are destined for slaughter; tigers living in a roadside zoo's deplorable conditions need immediate rescue and shelter. These stories are a mere glimpse of what is asked of just one sanctuary in a given week(“Why we Need Animal Sanctuaries OPED”).

These animals are taken from horrible circumstances like the ones stated above, there's no other place for them besides the homes these genouse people have created.
